Biography

Eric Kibet is a dedicated Clinical Officer currently serving at Kibabii University Hospital, where he provides comprehensive healthcare services to students, staff, and the surrounding community. He holds a Diploma in Clinical Medicine and is presently advancing his expertise by pursuing a Higher Diploma in Family Health at the Kenya Medical Training College (KMTC).

With a strong foundation in primary healthcare, disease prevention, and clinical management, Eric is passionate about improving access to quality medical care, promoting health education, and addressing the diverse needs of families. His professional journey reflects a commitment to continuous learning, teamwork, and patient-centered care.

Beyond his clinical practice, Eric takes interest in community outreach, mentoring young healthcare trainees, and exploring innovations in family health and preventive medicine. His long-term goal is to specialize further in family and community health, contributing to the strengthening of Kenya's healthcare system.
